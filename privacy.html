Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ProofPal (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the ProofPal mobile app and Apple Watch app (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Content you create: recipes, timers, fermentation sessions, and related notes.
  • App settings: notification preferences and in-app configuration.
  • Device/app data: limited diagnostics and crash/performance data provided by Apple.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or advertising identifiers unless explicitly stated in a future update.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide timer, stretch/fold, baking, and reminder functionality.
  • To sync data across your devices when Apple/iCloud sync features are enabled.
  • To improve app stability and performance.

3. Notifications, Live Activities, and Watch Features

ProofPal uses local notifications, Live Activities, and Apple Watch connectivity to deliver reminders and haptics. Notification behavior depends on your device settings (sound, silent mode, focus modes, and watch routing).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with service providers necessary to run the App, such as Apple services (for example, iCloud/CloudKit sync and system notification delivery), when those features are enabled by you.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained while you use the App. You can delete app data from within the App (where available) or by deleting the App and its iCloud data through your Apple account settings.

6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal data. Contact us to submit a request.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@proofpal.app
