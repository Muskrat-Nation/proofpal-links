ProofPal Support

Welcome to the official support page for ProofPal. This page covers what the app does, practical usage advice, and answers to common questions.

App Overview

ProofPal is designed to help you proofread and improve written content quickly. It focuses on readability, correctness, and clear communication so you can publish with confidence.

Use ProofPal for short notes, emails, reports, or long-form drafts whenever you want a second pass before sharing your writing.

General Advice

  • Review suggestions one by one so your original tone stays intact.
  • For best results, proofread in short sections instead of very large blocks.
  • Run a final read-through out loud to catch phrasing that still feels awkward.
  • Keep your app updated to receive the latest improvements and fixes.

FAQs

What does ProofPal help with?

ProofPal helps improve grammar, spelling, punctuation, clarity, and overall flow.

Does ProofPal replace manual editing?

No. It is best used as a smart assistant. A quick manual final review is still recommended.

How can I get better corrections?

Provide complete sentences and enough context. Clear inputs lead to better edits.

Where do I report a bug or ask for help?

Contact support using the email below and include what you were doing plus any error details.
